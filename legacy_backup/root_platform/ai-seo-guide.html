1. What is AI SEO?

AI SEO refers to the process of optimizing digital content specifically for large language models and generative search engines. It involves structuring information so that AI crawlers can easily parse, categorize, and synthesize your data into direct answers. Unlike traditional SEO, which focuses on keyword rankings, AI SEO prioritizes topical authority and factual accuracy.

2. What is GEO optimization?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization, a new standard for ensuring visibility in AI-driven search results. It requires technical enhancements like clear entity linking, structured data markup, and the use of natural language patterns. Effective GEO strategies focus on making content highly relevant for LLM citation algorithms.

3. How to rank in ChatGPT search?

To rank in ChatGPT search, your content must be cited as a credible source by the underlying retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system. This is achieved by publishing high-quality, expert articles with clear citations and structured definitions. Maintaining a strong digital footprint across reputable directories and forums also increases the likelihood of being referenced by AI.

4. How to improve AI visibility?

Improving AI visibility requires a combination of technical structured data (JSON-LD) and semantically enriched content. You should use clear headers, bullet points, and definitive statements that AI models can easily extract. Frequent content updates and maintaining high E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness) signals are essential for long-term AI visibility.

5. What is AI search indexing?

AI search indexing is the process by which generative engines convert web content into vector embeddings for inclusion in their knowledge bases. This differs from traditional indexing as it captures the semantic meaning and context of the text rather than just keywords. Optimized content is more likely to be retrieved and synthesized during user queries in AI chat interfaces.
