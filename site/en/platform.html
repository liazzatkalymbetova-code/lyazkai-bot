Frequently Asked Questions

What is the InfoLady platform?

InfoLady is an AI-first digital visibility platform combining a website SEO and GEO audit tool, a search trend radar, and a curated digital tools catalog. It helps businesses get found in both traditional and AI-powered search engines.

How is InfoLady different from regular SEO tools?

Traditional SEO tools focus on Google rankings. InfoLady also optimizes for AI-generated answers in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini. We analyze 50+ signals including AI citability, llms.txt, AI crawler access, and structured data quality.

Is the visibility audit really free?

Yes. The basic visibility scan is free and shows your critical issues instantly. A full detailed audit report with 50+ signals and a prioritized weekly action plan is available as a paid upgrade starting at $50.

Does InfoLady work for small businesses with no technical staff?

Absolutely. The audit results use plain language, not developer jargon. You get specific instructions like "Add this schema tag to your homepage" — not "improve your canonical structure." Most clients implement the first round of fixes themselves.

What is GEO, and why does it matter?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization — the practice of making your content easy for AI systems to discover, parse, and cite. A 2024 study by Georgia Tech, Princeton, and IIT Delhi found that GEO-optimized content receives 30–115% more AI-generated citations. It's the next frontier in digital visibility.
