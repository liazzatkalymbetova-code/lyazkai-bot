Why Most Websites Are Invisible to Customers

The way people search for products and services has fundamentally changed. In 2024–2026, AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews now answer up to 40% of commercial queries directly — without sending users to websites at all. If your site is not optimized for AI search (GEO), your competitors are capturing those customers instead.

Traditional SEO is not enough

Ranking on page 1 of Google no longer guarantees traffic. AI search engines pull answers directly from websites — but only from those they can understand and cite.

Slow sites lose customers instantly

53% of users abandon a website that takes more than 3 seconds to load on mobile. Every second of delay costs 7% in conversions on average.

Weak content = zero AI citations

AI engines like Perplexity and ChatGPT cite websites that have clear answer blocks, structured data, and E-E-A-T signals. Generic content is invisible to them.

No visibility score = guesswork

Without a measurable score, you cannot know if your SEO improvements are working. InfoLady quantifies every dimension of your visibility in a single dashboard.

What InfoLady Analyzes (50+ Signals)

Our AI scanner was built specifically for the 2025–2026 search landscape — where traditional SEO metrics alone no longer predict visibility.

⚡ Core Web Vitals

LCP, INP, CLS — the three metrics Google uses to rank pages by user experience. Poor scores directly reduce your rankings and increase bounce rate.

🏷️ Schema.org Markup

Organization, Article, FAQPage, Product, BreadcrumbList, and speakable schemas. These are the primary signals AI search engines use to understand and cite your content.

📱 Mobile-First Check

Over 70% of search queries now come from mobile devices. We verify responsive breakpoints, tap target sizes, font legibility, and viewport configuration.

🔍 AI Crawler Access

We check if GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, and Google-Extended can access your site — and verify your robots.txt, meta robots, and llms.txt configuration.

📖 Content Citability

We score each content block on 5 dimensions: answer quality, self-containment, statistical density, structural readability, and uniqueness — exactly how AI models evaluate sources.

🏢 Entity Authority

We analyze your brand's presence across platforms that AI models use for entity verification: Wikipedia, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, industry directories, and social profiles.
