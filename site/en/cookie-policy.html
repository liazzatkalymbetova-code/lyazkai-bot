Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and provide analytics data.

2. How InfoLady Uses Cookies and Local Storage

We use a combination of browser cookies and localStorage to:

  • Maintain your login session (localStorage: infolady_session)
  • Store your scan history and leads (localStorage: infolady_leads)
  • Remember language preference (EN / RU)
  • Collect anonymous analytics (Google Analytics)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie / Storage Key Type Purpose Duration
infolady_session localStorage User login session Until logout
infolady_users localStorage Account credentials (hashed) Until cleared
infolady_leads localStorage Scan history and leads Until cleared
lang_pref localStorage Language preference Until cleared
_ga Cookie Google Analytics (anonymous) 2 years
_gid Cookie Google Analytics (session) 24 hours

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services used by InfoLady may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analysis
  • PayPal — payment processing during checkout
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies and localStorage through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy

Note: Clearing localStorage will log you out and remove your scan history.

6. Your Consent

By using InfoLady, you consent to the use of cookies and localStorage as described in this policy. Essential functionality cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the platform to work.
