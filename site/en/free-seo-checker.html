What is an SEO Checker?

A plain-English explanation of what website SEO analysis does and why it matters.

An SEO Checker is a tool that scans a website and measures how well it is optimized for search engines like Google. It looks at technical signals, on-page content, link structure, page speed, mobile usability, and more — then gives you a score out of 100 and shows what needs fixing.

The InfoLady Free SEO Checker goes a step further by also measuring AI Visibility — how likely your site is to be cited in ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity. This is called GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free SEO checker really free? +

Yes — completely free. No signup, no credit card, no hidden fees. Enter any website URL and get instant SEO results.

What does the SEO checker analyze? +

It analyzes four dimensions: SEO Score (50+ on-page signals), Technical Health (page speed, CWV, mobile, HTTPS), AI Visibility — our GEO score for ChatGPT & Google AI Overviews — and Content Quality (readability, keyword density, E-E-A-T).

What is AI Visibility (GEO score)? +

AI Visibility (GEO — Generative Engine Optimization score) measures how likely your site is to be cited by AI search engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity. It is based on structured data quality, semantic content clarity, and entity recognition signals.

How is this free checker different from the full audit? +

The free checker gives a quick overview with 4 scores and your top detected issues. The AI SEO Audit is a full 5-section PDF report with exact fix instructions, 3-competitor benchmarking, content recommendations, and a prioritized action plan ordered by impact.

How accurate are the SEO scores? +

Scores are generated by AI analysis of publicly available website signals and provide directionally accurate benchmarks. They show where your site stands relative to best practices. For the most precise analysis and specific fixes, the professional audit is recommended.
